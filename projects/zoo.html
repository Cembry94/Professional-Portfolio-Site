Welcome to our Zoo Animal Information page!

Bears

  • Ollie

    Ollie is a friendly bear who loves honey and playing in the water.

  • Mona

    Mona is a curious bear who loves exploring her surroundings.

Giraffes

  • Frankie

    Frankie is a tall and majestic giraffe known for his long neck

  • Coconut"

    Coconut is a playful young giraffe who loves to interact with zoo visitors. She's especially fond of the treats they bring her.

Lions

  • Mella

    Mella is a majestic lioness known for her graceful presence and strong leadership in the pride. She enjoys basking in the sun and overseeing her territory.

  • Karl

    Karl, the king of the pride, is known for his powerful roar and commanding presence. He plays a key role in protecting the pride and its territory.

Monkeys

  • Cookie

    Cookie is a mischievous monkey known for his love of treats and playful antics. He's particularly fond of swinging from branch to branch and interacting with Zoo visitors.

  • Earl

    Earl is a wise old monkey who enjoys observing his surroundings from a distance. With age, he has become the voice of reason among the younger monkeys.

  • Banana

    Banana Pudding, fondly named for his lover of bananas, is a jovial monkey. He's known for his infectious laughter and cheerful demeanor, making him a favorite among children visiting the zoo.

Alligators

  • Wren

    Wren is a formidable alligator, recognized by her distinct markings and powerful jaws. She is often seen basking in the sun of gliding effortlessly through the water.

  • Aspen

    Aspen, known for his agile movements, is a younger alligator with a curious nature. He's particularly interested in the fish that share his enclosure.

  • Mika

    Mika, with her piercing eyes and swift reflexes, is a master hunter. She's an expert at camouflage, often surprising her prey from beneath the water's surface.
